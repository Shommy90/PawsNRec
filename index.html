Say fur-well to bored, anxious, stressed, and depressed

Keep your pup happy, healthy, entertained and out of trouble with an exclusive membership-only solution that exercises not only their bodies, but also their minds.

Many urbanites find it hard to provide their furry family members with the level of stimulation and activity that dogs in the suburbs are accustomed to. When our pups don’t get the physical, mental and social stimulation they need and deserve, our best friends can quickly become enemies. From chewing, digging and the good ol’ “what’s that smell”, to compulsive barking and outright disobedience, scenarios like these are far too common amongst city-dwelling pet owners. That’s why we set out to establish a fresh approach to modern pet care – Paws ‘n’ Rec: an unconventional pet enrichment, training and adventure company.

Our Services

  • PLAYCARE & ENRICHMENT

    The perfect combo of academics and athletics, whether on-site or off on one of our many local adventures, each day your pup will experience the ultimate enrichment trifecta of basic obedience, exercise and socialization.

  • ADVENTURES

    With dog parks, beaches, trails and events galore, our weekly off-site adventures ensure your dog doesn't miss out on the chance to roam, sniff, play and explore all of the dog-friendly fun and adventures our beautiful city has to offer.

  • TRAINING

    Whether a first-time learner, in need of a refresher, or looking to expand knowledge, your best friend will be a canine Einstein in no time once they team up with our certified, veterinarian-endorsed trainers.

  • OVERNIGHT CARE

    From our Bark Plaza Studios™ to Fur-Seasons Suites™, you and your fur-child will rest easy thanks to our state-of-the-art clean, comfortable and cage-free luxury accommodations and 24/7 personalized care.
